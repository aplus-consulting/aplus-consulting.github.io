Meet the Founder

Dr. Shervin Sammak, the founder of A+ Consulting, LLC, is a seasoned professional with a profound passion for technology and innovation. He is an engineer by training as he holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Aerospace Engineering and two Master of Science degress in Aerospace and Petroleum Engineering. He also earned his Ph.D. in Computational Modeling and Simulation from the University of Pittsburgh.

Dr. Sammak's academic prowess laid the foundation for an illustrious career that seamlessly blends academia and industry. During his time as a professor at the University of Pittsburgh, Dr. Sammak dedicated five years to teaching and mentoring both undergraduate and graduate students. He offered a diverse range of numerical courses, imparting invaluable knowledge to the next generation of aspiring engineers and scientists.

With a burning desire to bridge the gap between theory and practice, he ventured into the world of industry. At Dell Technologies, he has had the privilege of serving as a Senior Product Manager, leading the development and launch of groundbreaking HPC and AI solutions. Through engaging customers and forging strategic partnerships, He have driven substantial revenue growth and market expansion.
